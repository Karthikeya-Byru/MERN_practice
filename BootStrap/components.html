Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, often called "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is a fictional character from the popular manga and anime series One Piece. He is a highly skilled swordsman and the second member to join Monkey D. Luffy's crew, the Straw Hat Pirates. Zoro's dream is to become the world's greatest swordsman, and he is known for his three-sword style, which he calls "Santoryu".
